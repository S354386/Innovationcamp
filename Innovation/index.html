How can we secure education for the next generation

We want all children to get the oppurtunity to learn and educate themselves in order to improve their lives.
The hardest part is to find ways that catch children who falls outside of the education system.
We want to create a soulution that will help goverments and charitable foundation track people who falls off or behind on their education.
We have therefor come up with a solution that will make it easy for educators to report students that fail to show up to shcool or who falls behind on subjects.
Since techonlogy can be scarse and hard to aquire for some places, we will try to use current techonlogy that already exist most places like phones.
You can either send in the information about attendance by mail to a regional office who will then input it online on a computer,
or send the information in by sms.
